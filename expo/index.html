Executive Summary

Turkey Expo Kenya 2026 is a four-day national event organized by Pinamba Investment Group, bringing together the best of Turkish fashion, décor, and textile innovation. It's where creativity meets opportunity, connecting manufacturers, designers, traders, and investors in one vibrant space to celebrate Turkish elegance and craftsmanship right here in Kenya.

Beyond the exhibition halls awaits an unforgettable adventure. Guests will experience Kenya's breathtaking safaris, from the golden plains of the Maasai Mara to the serene beauty of the coast. It's more than sightseeing; it's a journey through nature, culture, and connection, creating memories that last long after the business deals are done.

In the spirit of "killing two birds with one stone," the Turkey Expo Kenya 2026 blends business with leisure. We build partnerships, explore new markets, and enjoy Kenya's finest leisure destinations. It's not just an expo; it's an experience where trade meets travel and business meets pleasure.

Even in the face of global economic challenges, the Turkey Expo Kenya 2026 invites you to rediscover opportunity and adventure. You come for business and experience leisure through Kenya's iconic safaris and coastal escapes, where every moment combines discovery, connection, and culture. It's where enterprise meets exploration, proving that growth can be both productive and unforgettable.

1. Background and Objectives

Kenya's fashion and textile industry continues to expand, driven by a rising appreciation for local creativity and quality craftsmanship. However, niche segments like turkey-themed fashion and décor materials remain underrepresented in mainstream markets.

The Turkey Expo Kenya 2026 aims to fill this gap by establishing a premier platform to promote turkey-based clothing, home décor, and textile innovation.

General Objectives

1
Establish the Turkey Treasures Mall as a premier retail destination for Turkish products in Kenya
2
Promote the turkey clothing and materials industry in Kenya
3
Create a national platform for local turkey wear traders, manufacturers, and designers
4
Attract investors and business partners in fashion, textiles, and home décor
5
Showcase innovations in turkey material production
6
Stimulate market linkages and partnerships
7
Highlight technological innovation in textile and apparel
8
Encourage Kenya-Turkey tech partnerships

Core Objective

The establishment and development of the proposed Turkey Treasures Mall as a premier retail destination for Turkish products in Kenya, serving as a central marketplace, logistics hub, training center, and tourism attraction.

Proposed Turkey Treasures Mall

The Expo serves as a strategic launchpad for Turkey Treasures Mall, which will be a landmark retail and exhibition destination in Nairobi that will showcase premium Turkish clothing, materials, carpets, and crafts. Our engagements with both Kenyan and Turkish investors have generated significant interest, with many expressing readiness to finance the mall's construction.

Investors from both countries have further demonstrated flexibility, showing willingness to set up the mall at any optimal location in Nairobi. We invite additional partners who believe in this vision and are eager to be part of a high-potential, cross-border investment opportunity. The project will adopt a transparent shareholding structure, ensuring equitable ownership and strong returns for all stakeholders.

We continue to welcome additional investors who share and support this vision. Ownership of the mall will be structured on a shareholding basis, ensuring equitable participation among all partners.

The mall will serve as:

MKT

Central Marketplace

A premier retail hub for local and regional traders, offering curated collections and establishing new market standards.

LOG

Logistics Hub

Host Turkish offices like Travel Agents, airlines and logistics offices for import and exports between Kenya and Turkey.

TRN

Training Center

Designs and skills acquisition center for those seeking employment and study opportunities in Turkey.

TRS

Tourism Attraction

Showcasing Kenyan creativity and craftsmanship with Turkish restaurants and cultural experiences for visitors.
